# ✍️ Inkwell: Narrative Intelligence & AI Writing Assistant

> **An AI-powered writing assistant that understands stories, not just sentences. Built during the 24-hour DevHacks CodeCraft Hackathon to enhance long-form writing through narrative intelligence, contextual reasoning, and explainable AI.**

## 🌍 Overview

**Inkwell** is a Narrative Intelligence and Writing Assistant developed during **DevHacks CodeCraft**, a 24-hour hackathon hosted by **Atharva College of Engineering**.

Built for the **AI-Powered Writer – Script & Content Enhancement System** problem statement, Inkwell goes beyond traditional grammar correction by understanding the overall narrative context of a document. It analyzes characters, timelines, locations, relationships, and plot progression to identify inconsistencies and generate meaningful, context-aware writing suggestions.

Instead of relying solely on prompt engineering, the project incorporates a custom **Narrative Intelligence Pipeline** featuring semantic chunking, knowledge graph-based context tracking, and an alert-driven recommendation system to provide transparent and explainable outputs.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 📖 Narrative consistency tracking across long-form content
- 👥 Character, timeline, and relationship analysis
- 🧠 Context-aware content enhancement suggestions
- 🎭 Style and tone transformation (Formal, Academic, Cinematic, etc.)
- 🔍 Explainable AI outputs showing what changed and why
- 📊 Plot thread visualization for improved story understanding
- ✍️ Interactive writing editor with side-by-side comparison and real-time insights
